# Shopify Abandoned Cart Apps

Agent-readable guide for abandoned-cart research.

Choose by event freshness, cart and checkout identity, recovery timing, product context, discount controls, consent, deliverability, and reporting. Keep cart recovery distinct from transactional order communication and SMS permissions. Define the purchase event as the hard suppression rule before enrollment.

Use official vendor and Shopify sources for current claims. Attribute vendor case studies and avoid converting reported recovered revenue into a universal promise. Measure incremental purchase behavior, margin, unsubscribe, complaint, and discount cost.
